About

The Atisundara Foundation provides funding for peacebuilding projects to promote reconciliaton and healing from past conflict. Activities funded by the foundation include insight meditation in the Therevada Buddhist Tradition and the protection and preservation of endangered languages and cultures.

    • Alex Young, MPA, GPLLM

    Director

    Alex Young draws from a 20-year career in law and finance negotiating commercial contracts in real estate development and other sectors of the economy. He is a lawyer in good standing of the Law Society of Ontario with a special interest in construction contracts and performance guarantees.

    Expertise


    Alex is Co-Founder and a pro-bono teacher in residence at the Buddhavipassana Meditation Centre in Toronto. His work in developing communities centred on insight meditation was recognized in 2014 by the Governor of Chiangmai Province and by Thailand's federal Ministry of Culture, who appointed him a Benefactor of Buddhism in 2020.
