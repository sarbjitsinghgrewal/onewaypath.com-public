About

The Atisundara Foundation provides funding for peacebuilding projects to promote reconciliaton and healing from past conflict. Activities funded by the foundation include insight meditation in the Therevada Buddhist Tradition and the protection and preservation of endangered languages and cultures.

    • Miriam Young, H. BKin, H. BScN, RN

    Director

    A registered nurse for over 12 years, Miriam applies her years of experience in the health care industry to community design. Her expertise integrates food security, social inclusion, access to health services and opportunities for work-life balance into the design from the start of the process.

    Expertise


    Miriam’s work and dedication to peace building though insight meditation has been recognized by Thai governments both at the national and provincial levels. She is a recipient of the benefactor of Buddhism award from the governor of Chiangmai Province in 2013 and from the Princess of Thailand in 2018. She is co-founder and pro-bono teacher in residence at the Buddhavipassana Meditation Centre in Toronto.
