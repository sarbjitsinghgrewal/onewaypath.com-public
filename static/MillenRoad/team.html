About

One Way Path Communities builds multi-residential housing in liveable communities in and around the Greater Toronto Hamilton Area. These projects present an opportunity for equity investors to earn high rates of return with limited risk. Intrested equity and debt partners are invited to contact info@onewaypath.com for more information.

    • Team

    One Way Path Communities brings together an inter-disciplinary team of professionals with experience in community builiding, human design and construction.

    ALEX YOUNG
    President & Chief Executive Officer

    Alex draws from a 20-year career in law and finance negotiating commercial contracts in real estate development and other sectors of the economy. He is a lawyer in good standing of the Law Society of Ontario with a special interest in construction contracts and performance guarantees.

    MIRIAM YOUNG
    Vice President & Chief Design Officer

    A registered nurse for over 12 years, Miriam applies her years of experience in the health care industry to community design. Her expertise integrates food security, social inclusion, access to health services and opportunities for work-life balance into the design from the start of the process.

    SAM SPAGNUOLO
    Lead Architect

    Sam is a partner at award winning CS&P Architects. An expert at creative approaches to maximizing saleable area and cost efficiency, Sam has designed and coordinated construction of over 600 multiresidential units across the Greater Toronto-Hamilton Area.

    JAMES SAMUEL
    Lead Structural Engineer

    James is Principal of J.P. Samuel & Associates, a structural engineering firm based in Hamilton, Ontario. He has extensive experience in a wide range of asset classes including large multi-family residential projects.

    ROBERT WALTER-JOSEPH
    City Planner

    Good planning improves communities and increases property values. Robert, a Senior Planner at Gladki Planning Associates, has spent 7 years working on land use and public policy planning. He applies his experience in Greater Toronto-Hamilton area multi-family housing projects to make the development a place people want to live, work and socialize.

    BRIAN VERSPAGAN
    Lead Civil Engineer

    A partner at WalterFedy civil engineers, Brian has experience in different types of construction including multi-family developments. With an office in Hamilton, Brian's team knows how to maximize existing city service connections for the development.
